What kind of cloud meetings can we capture? Pretty much everything!

  • Zoom cloud recordings
  • Zoom cloud transcripts (if available)
  • Teams cloud recordings (via Outlook plugin)
  • Teams cloud transcripts (if available, via Outlook plugin))
  • Google Meet (via Zapier)
  • Dropbox (any audio or video recordings)

Meet the Myna recording app

If you’re a Windows 10 user, you can capture any meeting.

  • Integrates with GSuite/Office365 Calendar
  • Auto-start/stop selected meetings
  • Record by meeting type or calendar label
  • Upload via Dropbox to Myna (Google Drive coming soon)
  • Add Hot Button markers in real-time

Connect to calendar

Myna adds a link in your Outlook calendar to your meeting SmartTranscript. You can also capture recorded Teams meetings via Stream. Add additional meeting details direct to your SmartTranscript.
